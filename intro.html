• Clear operational pain points (e.g., outdated layouts, zero search visibility).

Negative Lead Signal (Drop Immediately)

• Low economic bandwidth or intense friction over micro-payments.

• No clear decision authority paths (stuck inside endless lower corporate management layers).

• Zero understanding of marketing leverage—views performance spend purely as an expense.

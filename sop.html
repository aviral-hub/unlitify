SOP 1: Lead Generation

KPI: Leads/Month • CPL • Traffic

Operational Process

  • Publish SEO blog content matrix.
  • Programmatic Posting: LinkedIn, Instagram, X (Twitter).
  • Execute Performance Campaigns: Meta / Google Ads.
  • Convert Traffic via Website Intake Forms.
  • Route verified leads into CRM system.
  • Execute immediate triage: Assign to Sales < 30 minutes.

Objective

Systematically generate and capture qualified inbound lead velocity consistently over defined sprint cycles.

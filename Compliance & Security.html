USA (Tax & Legal)

03. Performance Terms

Settlement Protocol

  • Net-0 Settlement: All management fees are due upfront to initiate infrastructure deployment.

04. Anti-Poaching

Team Integrity Clause

Client agrees not to solicit, hire, or engage any Unlitify partners or contractors directly for a period of 24 months following the termination of this agreement. Violation incurs a liquidated damages fee of 50% of the annual partner compensation.

By initiating a project with Unlitify, you agree to these protocols.
